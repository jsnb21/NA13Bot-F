Training File Guidelines
  • PDF/DOCX: Add clear headings so related details stay together.
  • TXT/JSON/CSV: Write clear short paragraphs.
  • Helpful Labels: Keep names like "Invoice #123" or "Section 4.2" if they matter.
  • Clarity: Keep related facts close to each other.
How File Reading Works
  • Automatic: The app checks the file and decides the best way to read it.
  • For PDF/DOCX: Heading and related text are kept together.
  • For TXT-like files: Long text is split into small connected parts.
  • Extra Details: We keep useful tags like file type, page number, and section name.
Best Practices
  • For size variants, keep one item name and write Options: Small=120, Medium=140, Large=160. Put the lowest price in the main Price field.
  • Use clear, consistent formatting
  • Include context for Q&A pairs
  • Update files regularly
  • Keep file sizes under 50MB
  • Avoid duplicate content
Simple Training File Example

Use clear section titles and simple wording in .txt, .docx, or .pdf files. No coding needed.

1. Restaurant Details

Restaurant Name: Scott's Restaurant

Address: 123 Main St, Quezon City

Contact Number: +63 912 345 6789

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM (Daily)

Delivery Areas: Quezon City, San Juan

Special Notes: We accept dine-in, takeout, and delivery.

2. Menu Items

Category: Main Course

Item: Beef Tapa

Price: 180

Description: Marinated beef strips served with garlic rice and egg.

Category: Drinks/Beverages

Item: Iced Latte

Price: 120

Description: Options: Small=120, Medium=140, Large=160

Note: Keep one item only for variants. Do not create separate items like Iced Latte Small and Iced Latte Large.

Q: What time do you open?

A: We open daily at 10:00 AM.

Q: Do you offer delivery?

A: Yes, we deliver within selected nearby areas.

Q: What are your bestsellers?

A: Our bestsellers are Beef Tapa and Iced Latte.
