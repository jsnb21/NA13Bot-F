How it Works
  • Generate: Enter the number of tables and starting number, then click "Generate"
  • Preview: QR codes will appear below. Each code links to your chatbot with the table number
  • Print: Click "Print QR Codes" to open the print preview. Print in color for best scanning
  • Placement: Place printed QR codes on each table or at the entrance
  • Scanning: When customers scan, they're taken to your chatbot with their table number already set
  • Local Network: QR codes automatically work on any device connected to your local network
